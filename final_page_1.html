KPOP Choreographies

K-pop choreography is a combination of hip-hop and jazz
choreographed to Korean pop music.
K-pop choreography often includes catchy and repetitive movements that complement the song's lyrics.
Some say that K-pop choreography is hard because the dance steps
need to be powerful enough to catch the audience's eyes.
K-pop groups usually have two weeks or more to master their dance routines.
